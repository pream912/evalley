Services

We offer following services

Custom ERP

A tailored software solution that is designed to meet the unique needs and requirements of a specific organization. It can provide a comprehensive and integrated approach to managing business processes, data, and resources, increasing efficiency and productivity while reducing costs.

IT Consultation

IT consultation involves providing expert advice and guidance on the selection, implementation, and optimization of ERP systems. It helps organizations to maximize the benefits of ERP by aligning their business processes with the software and identifying areas for improvement.

IT Infra Management

Involves the planning, design, deployment, monitoring, and maintenance of an organization's IT infrastructure. It encompasses various technologies, such as servers, networks, storage, and security, and ensures their optimal performance and availability to support business operations.

Project information

  • Category: Custom ERP
  • Client: Mohammed Mustafa & Samsuddin Co. Pte Ltd
  • Project date: 01 March, 2020

Customised ERP Solution to manage Inventory, Point of Sales, Whole Sale with integrated Accounting System.

The customer has more than 300 thousands unique SKU selling in their retail store. Our system manage online stock updation, stock transfer from various locations, POS billing, Cashier Schedule management, Customer management, Vendor management, Day end closing, transactions posting to accounts, bank reconciliation and accounts. Produce lot analysis reports for the management for better decision making.

Customised Invoice and Accounting system.

They are doing Engineering Services in Marine industry. The customer facing difficulty to manage Engineer Schedules, tool management, quotation, invoicing and accounts. Before the system customer prepare quotation for their customer's requested job. Normally it took more than 3 working days to working out the cost for the Job. Lot of missing items included in the quotes to the customer due to manual process and also no option to validate the tools and analysis the Job status. The newer system streamlines the operation from Quotation to Billing and manpower and accounts. Manage the Job process and produce lot analysis reports for the management for better decision making. The newer system improved the productivity of operation over 50%.
